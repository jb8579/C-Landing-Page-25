Cookie Policy

Introduction

This cookie policy explains how our website uses cookies and similar technologies to provide you with a better experience. By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, enhance functionality, and collect statistics to optimize the user experience.

Why do we use cookies?

We use cookies for several reasons, including:

  • Essential cookies: To enable core functionalities like navigation and access to secure areas of the website.
  • Performance cookies: To understand how users interact with our website so we can improve it.
  • Functional cookies: To remember your preferences and personalize your experience.
  • Advertising cookies: To display relevant ads and measure their effectiveness.

Types of cookies we use

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: Cookies that remain on your device until they expire or are manually deleted.
  • First-party cookies: Cookies placed directly by our website.
  • Third-party cookies: Cookies placed by external parties, such as for analytics or advertising.

Managing cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Here, you can block or delete cookies. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect some website functionalities.

  • For Google Chrome: Chrome cookies management guide
  • For Mozilla Firefox: Firefox cookies management guide
  • For Microsoft Edge: Edge cookies management guide
  • For Safari: Safari cookies management guide

Third-party cookies

We collaborate with third parties who may place cookies on your device through our website. These cookies are used for analytics, advertising, or other services they provide. We have no control over these cookies and recommend reading their privacy policies for more information.

Changes to our cookie policy

We reserve the right to change this cookie policy at any time. Any changes will take effect once they are published on our website. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed.
